Element diggs_geo:StaticConePenetrationTest
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/2.6/geotechnical
Annotations
 
An object describing the properties of an in-situ static cone test procedure. This Test is an in situ testing method used to determine
the geotechnical engineering properties of soils and delineating soil stratigraphy.
The test method consists of pushing an instrumented cone, with the tip facing down,
into the ground at a controlled rate.
