Element diggs_geo:LabPermeationTrial / diggs_geo:deltaHgC
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/3/geotechnical
Annotations
 
The difference in elevation of mercury in the
headwater and tailwater tubes of the manometer with equal
pressures applied to both tubes. This value is positive if the
inside diameter of the headwater tube is larger than the inside
of the tailwater tube, and negative if the opposite is true.
