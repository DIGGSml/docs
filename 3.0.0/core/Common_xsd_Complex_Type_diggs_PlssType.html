Element diggs:PlssType / diggs:governmentLot
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/3
Annotations
 
The name of a subpart of a section which is not described
as an aliquot part of the section, but which is designated by
number, for example, Lot 3. A lot may be regular or irregular in
shape, and its acreage may vary from that of regular aliquot parts.
These lots frequently border water areas excluded from the
PLSS.
