Element diggs:DirectShearTest
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/3
Annotations
 
An object describing a direct shear test procedure. The direct shear
test, also known as a shearbox test, is used for determination of the consolidated
drained (or undrained) shear strength of soils. The test is performed by deforming a
specimen at a controlled rate on or near a single shear plane.
