Facets
enumeration (mass per volume) per pressure 
(mass per volume) per pressure: Rate of change of density with pressure. Used in compressible fluid mechanics, formation evaluation, and soil compressibility studies. Represents the piezometric coefficient or how material density responds to pressure changes. Common in consolidation analysis and high-pressure geotechnical testing. Base unit equivalent: kg/(m³·Pa) or (kg/m³)/Pa.
enumeration (mass per volume) per temperature 
(mass per volume) per temperature: Rate of change of density with temperature. Used in thermal fluid mechanics, materials science, and geothermal analysis. Represents the thermal expansion coefficient expressed as density change per degree. Important in thermally-induced ground movement analysis and thermal properties of materials. Base unit equivalent: kg/(m³·K) or (kg/m³)/K.
enumeration area per count 
area per count: Area per discrete entity or particle. Used in nuclear physics for cross-sections, particle analysis, microscopy, and discrete event analysis. The barn (10⁻²⁸ m²) is a common unit in nuclear applications. Important in particle size distribution analysis and nuclear logging interpretation. Base unit equivalent: m²/count or barn/particle.
enumeration diffusive time of flight 
diffusive time of flight: Square root of time parameter. Used in diffusion analysis, consolidation theory, and transient flow processes where behavior follows square-root-of-time relationships. This unusual quantity class represents the time scale for diffusive processes. Common in Terzaghi consolidation analysis and diffusion-controlled phenomena. Base unit equivalent: s^0.5 or √s.
enumeration pressure per pressure 
pressure per pressure: Dimensionless ratio of pressures. Used for normalized pressure, pressure ratios, safety factors in pressure-dependent systems, and gradient expressions. Common in pore pressure analysis, stress ratio calculations, and pressure transient interpretation. Important for expressing pressure changes relative to reference pressures. Base unit equivalent: Pa/Pa (dimensionless).
enumeration thermodynamic temperature per thermodynamic temperature 
thermodynamic temperature per thermodynamic temperature: Dimensionless ratio of temperatures. Used in thermodynamic calculations, thermal efficiency expressions, and normalized temperature parameters. Common in thermal analysis where temperatures are expressed relative to reference temperatures. Important in geothermal analysis and thermal property calculations. Base unit equivalent: K/K or °C/°C (dimensionless).
