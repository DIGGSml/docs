Element diggs:BackfillType / diggs:backfillType
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/3
Annotations
 
A controlled list of terms to indicate what type of
backfilling is described in the object. Choices are: annular space
within borehole - backfilling occurs within the annular space
between the outer casing and the natural borehole wall annular space
between casings - backfilling occurs within the annular space
between nested casings open hole - backfilling occurs within an open
hole within casing - backfilling occurs within the innermost
casing
