Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/2.6/geotechnical
Annotations
 
A test that detects total concentrations of organic gases and vapors, by ionizing the gases in a flame. A current is produced in proportion to the number of carbon atoms present. This test procedure is deprecated in favor of the EnvironmentalScreeningTest.
