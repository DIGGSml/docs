Element diggs:ComponentPropertiesType / diggs:rockGrainSize
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schema-dev
Annotations
 
A descriptive term or phrase to describe the overall
particle size or crystal size distribution of the rock, depending on
rock origin. For example, for cystalline igneous or metamorphic
rocks, terms such as pegmatitic or aphanitic may be used. For
clastic sedimentary rocks, terms such as fine sand or cobbles may be
used, and for pyroclastic igneous rocks, terms such as "coarse ash"
or "lapili" may be used. Intended to come from a controlled list of
terms.
