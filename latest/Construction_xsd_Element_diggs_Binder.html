Element diggs:Binder
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/2.6
Annotations
 
An object containing properties of the binder component of a grout mix,
including the supplier, specific gravity, type, volume, and weight of the component
used. Binder here refers to binding components of a grout mix exclusive of cement,
such as fly ash or lime.
