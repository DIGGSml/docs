Element diggs_geo:PressuremeterTest
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schemas/2.6/geotechnical
Annotations
 
The Pressuremeter measures both the strength and stress-strain properties of all soil types including soft rock. The pressuremeter is used primarily to calculate bearing capacity of shallow and deep foundations; settlement of foundations; deformation of laterally loaded and sheet piles and resistance of anchors
