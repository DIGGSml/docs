Element diggs:AggregateSlakeDurabilityTest
Namespace http://diggsml.org/schema-dev
Annotations
 
An object describing the properties of a slake durability test procedure. The Slake Durability Index predicts the long-term durability of the rock
specimens, to establish weathering and degradation characteristics of each rock
type, and to assess the impact of water on the rock degradation.
